Why do we sometimes miss?

Of the 3000 images of cereals we collected, we were only able to use and label roughly 1600 images for training. Given that there were way more images of cereals with high sugar content versus low and medium sugar content as well as some of the cereals using very similar images for multiple categories of sugar levels proved challenging. To improve, additional effort of collecting more images for training would help improve the model training.
